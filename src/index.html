About Us

Background of our company

  • Mission Statement
  • Our History
  • Our Target Market

Our initiative aims to provide underprivileged kids in rural areas with access to cloud computing resources and exposure to the tech industry. By partnering with local schools and community organizations, we offer a comprehensive program that includes hands-on learning experiences, curriculum and learning materials, and access to trained instructors and industry experts. Our goal is to help bridge the digital divide and prepare the next generation of tech leaders.

Young Cloud Explorers was founded in 2020 with a clear mission: to provide underprivileged learners between the ages of 16-18 with access to education and opportunities in cloud computing, Software programming and cybersecurity. Recognizing the transformative potential of technology, especially in the realm of cloud computing, our founders set out to bridge the digital divide and empower young minds to thrive in the digital age

Our target market is underprivileged kids in rural areas who may not have access to technology resources or exposure to the tech industry. We partner with local schools, community organizations, and other stakeholders to identify and reach out to eligible students.
